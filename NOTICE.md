## Generated item art (`assets/item-art/`)

The bundled item artwork was produced with OpenAI's `gpt-image-2` image model
via the pipeline in `scripts/art-pipeline.mjs`. Note that `gpt-image-2` does
not support deterministic seeding, so individual images cannot be regenerated
byte-for-byte from their prompts; the generation prompts and per-asset metadata
are recorded in `assets/item-art-plan.json`.
